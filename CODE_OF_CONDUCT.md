# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the SocQ public developer community a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity,
experience level, or personal characteristics.

## Our standards

Positive behavior includes being respectful, giving constructive feedback,
accepting responsibility, and focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, intimidation, discriminatory
language, publishing private information, or sustained disruption.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to [support@socq.ai](mailto:support@socq.ai).
The SocQ maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior
is inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.
